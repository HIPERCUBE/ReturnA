Writers aged over thirteen from all countries are
encouraged to enter East India Press Short Story Writing
Contest. You should submit your story by March 1, 2012.
There is no entry fee, and they accept fiction in any genre.
The contest is sponsored by East India Press, specializing in
the publishing of books in multiple formats. New York Times
bestselling author, David Farland, with over fifty published
novels, will be the judge. For more information, please visit
www.nightingalenovel.com.
