30. Pick up a water bottle, remove the label, and fill it with
any water that’s not too cloudy from a river or rainwater.
Place the bottle on a building’s metal roof in full sunlight. In
six hours the sun will kill viruses and bacteria in the water,
making it safe to drink. This method is now being used all
over the world to provide for some four
million people. “It’s simple, free, effective,” says a principal
in Tanzania. In 2006, her school started using the method to
clean its dirty tap water. The result? The rate of diseases
by water, such as diarrhea, dropped greatly.
