41. Few places on earth can match Madagascar as an
eco­tourism destination. About 70% of Madagascar’s animals
are found only in this island nation. But Madagascar hasn’t
always been great at displaying its biological richness. Its
tourism industry remains still small, but that’s beginning to
change. The current government is in the middle of tripling
the size of its national park system, and local­guide
networks are springing up around the country. These efforts
are coming at the right time, with “green” travel worldwide
growing three times as fast as the entire travel industry.
