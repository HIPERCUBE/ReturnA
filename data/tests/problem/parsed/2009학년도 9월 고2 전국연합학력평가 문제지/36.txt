36. Toco toucan에 관한 다음 글의 내용과 일치하는 것은?
The Toco toucan is at home in South America’s tropical
forests but can be seen everywhere. Its oversized, colorful
bill has made it one of the world’s most popular birds. The
toucan’s long bill is useful as a feeding tool. The bird uses
it to reach fruit on branches that are too small to sit on,
and also to peel the fruit. In addition to fruit, Toco toucans
eat insects and, sometimes, eggs or lizards. Although the
colorful bill looks nice and helps in eating, it is not a
practical weapon. While its size may be frightening to
enemies, it is of little use in combating them.
① 남미 지역 외에서는 찾아볼 수 없다.
② 작고 화려한 부리로 인해 인기가 있다.
③ 부리 때문에 나무 열매를 따먹는 것이 힘들다.
④ 나무 열매뿐만 아니라 곤충이나 도마뱀도 먹는다.
⑤ 부리는 적과 싸우는 아주 유용한 무기다.
