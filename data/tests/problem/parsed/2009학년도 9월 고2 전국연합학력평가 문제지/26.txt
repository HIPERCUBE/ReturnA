more inactive lifestyle has played a major role in the rise of
obesity. Two million years ago the expansion of the human
brain required energy­rich food. To find foods that are much
richer in calories and nutrients, our ancestors had to run and
hunt over large areas of land, meaning they burned up a lot
of the calories. With the shift to less active lifestyles,
humans now have a diet that contains far more calories than
they need to survive. Moreover, we can simply pick up the
