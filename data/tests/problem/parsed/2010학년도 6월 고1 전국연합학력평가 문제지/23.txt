23. 다음 글에서 전체 흐름과 관계 없는 문장은?
Some airlines were telling passengers on Saturday that
new government security regulations prohibit them from
leaving their seats an hour before landing. ①The regulations
are in response to a suspected terrorism incident on
Christmas Day. ②Air Canada said that the rules limit
on­board activities by passengers and crew in U.S. airspace.
③Advance Seat Selection Service depends on your booking
class or membership level. ④The airline said that during the
final hour of flight, passengers must remain seated. ⑤They
won’t be allowed access to carry­on baggage or to have any
items in their laps.
