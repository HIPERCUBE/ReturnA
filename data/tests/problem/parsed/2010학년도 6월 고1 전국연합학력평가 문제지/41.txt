41. Some genetically blessed people are born with the
complete potential for a brilliant smile. They have the right
genes for perfect teeth. But others don’t. These are the
ones who may need help from a dentist. The treatment to
obtain a brilliant smile may take up to four years. That is to
say, one might need to wear braces for two years and then
a retainer for two years to keep their teeth in place. For
one to acquire that brilliant smile, many procedures are
involved, and the patient’s cooperation is essential.
