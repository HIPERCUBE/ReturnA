31. 다음 글의 상황에 나타난 분위기로 가장 적절한 것은? [1점]
The sound was coming closer; he could hear the whisper
of leaves clearly off to his left. On the ground before him
was a stick. It was only a small branch, but he reached for
it anyway. He stripped the leaves from it and grasped it
tightly. Perhaps, he thought wildly, he could blind the beast
as it scratched him, trying to drag him from between the
rocks. How long would he have to wait? At last, without
further warning, the creature came into view. He felt his
pulse hammering in his throat. His thoughts seemed to be
running a desperate race with each other.
① funny ② urgent ③ festive
④ peaceful ⑤ monotonous
