43. 주어진 글 다음에 이어질 글의 순서로 가장 적절한 것은?
Most Americans eat lunch between noon and two o’clock.
This mid­day meal is eaten away from home more often
than breakfast or dinner.
(A) In addition, it is inexpensive and easy to prepare. It
needs only two pieces of bread and stuff to fill in
between.
(B) It means that working adults rarely go home for lunch,
and many schoolchildren also eat at school.
(C) Some of them bring food from home in a paper bag. For
this purpose, they need a meal that is small and portable.
The ‘sandwich’ meets these requirements.
① (A) - (C) - (B) ② (B) - (A) - (C)
③ (B) - (C) - (A) ④ (C) - (A) - (B)
⑤ (C) - (B) - (A)
