24. One of the most important aspects of green products
should be . If something is tough or can be
readily repaired, this lessens the chance that it’ll end up in
the landfill, and could easily save you money in the long
run, even if it’s initially more expensive. Even recyclable
materials, if they break and can’t be fixed, require energy
and other resources to reprocess and then replace.
Long­lasting goods can be passed on from person to person.
Even if your style changes and that kitchen table isn’t your
thing anymore, a good strong table will almost always be
appealing to someone else, while a broken and unfixable one
probably won’t.
