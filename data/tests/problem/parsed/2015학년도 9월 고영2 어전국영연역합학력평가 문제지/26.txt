Notice for All Guests
We hope to provide you with the best service possible.
Rates
• Our rates are seasonal. Please call or e­mail to inquire.
• 50% of full payment must be paid in advance to secure 
reservation.
Check­in & Check­out
• Check­in: 2:00p.m. -11:00p.m. 
• Check­out: by 11:00a.m.
• To extend your stay, check for availability before 10:00 a.m..
Services
• There’s a safe at the front desk to store your valuables. 
A charge of $2 will be added to your account.
• The computers in the lounge are only for searching the 
Internet. Please do not play computer games or download 
programs.
• Rooms are cleaned every other day. A $5 service charge 
will be added for daily cleaning.
