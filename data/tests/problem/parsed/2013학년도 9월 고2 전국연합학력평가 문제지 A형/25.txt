25. 다음 글의 요지로 가장 적절한 것은?
Have you ever noticed that some people can effortlessly
learn new concepts and materials while others struggle?
Some were born with extraordinarily high IQs, but certainly
not all. Fortunately, there is one technique that will help
you to learn faster, study better, and begin absorbing
information like a sponge. If there’s something you want to
learn, try teaching it to someone else. Traditional studying
helps you to memorize ideas, but teaching those ideas to
someone else forces you to truly “get” all of the concepts
and apply them to a number of solutions. To teach others
you must anticipate any potential questions and explore the
topic from all angles, dramatically increasing your own
understanding.
