2013 Victory High School’s
Holiday Toy Drive
What’s the best way to spread holiday
cheer? How about bringing smiles to the
faces of needy children by donating a
toy?
How to Participate
- The event will be held this Friday at
noon.
- Leave your donations in the drop box
at the school gym.
Toy’s Conditions
- Must be unbroken and clean
- Must be non-violent
Where Your Donation Goes
- Once the toys are collected, some
parents and teachers will deliver them
to the chosen charities.
Additional Info
- You can also donate your time by
helping to sort the toys.
- For more detailed information, please
visit our school website.
