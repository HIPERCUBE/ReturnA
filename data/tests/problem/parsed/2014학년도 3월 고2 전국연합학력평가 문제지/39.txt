39. 다음 글에서 전체 흐름과 관계 없는 문장은?
When you take a step, cross your legs, or bend to tie 
your shoe, your knees are working hard. ① Your knees take 
a great deal of stress every day, and you need to take good 
care of them. ② To increase your knee strength, you should 
boost the surrounding muscles, which act as a support 
system. ③ You can strengthen these muscles by doing 
exercises such as knee extensions. ④ The next time you go 
out walking or jogging, remember how hard your heart and 
lungs are working for you. ⑤ Maintaining a healthy weight 
and wearing proper footwear while walking or jogging will 
also help you maintain healthy knee joints.
