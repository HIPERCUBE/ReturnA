they spend time with― to pick up on their mind­sets,
beliefs, and approaches to challenges. When someone on
a team is rewarded by his good deeds, others are likely
to display similar characteristics. When a leader keeps

others admire that quality and want to be like her. When
a team member displays a strong work ethic and begins
to have a positive impact, others imitate him. Attitude is
catching: People become inspired by the good examples
of their peers.
