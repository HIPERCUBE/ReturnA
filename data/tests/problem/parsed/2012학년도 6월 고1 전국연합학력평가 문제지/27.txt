27. 다음 글에서 필자가 주장하는 바로 가장 적절한 것은?
When you cross paths with a stranger or accidentally catch
someone’s gaze, how do you react? You probably look away
in awkwardness or pretend you are looking at something
else. Well, why don’t you give them a big smile instead?
Deepak Chopra, an Indian medical doctor, talks about giving
each person you encounter a small gift. I do this with a
smile. Why not? It does not cost me anything. Plus, it’s a
lot more fun than feeling awkward and pretending I am not
there. When I smile at people who are not expecting it,
some blush, and others are surprised and smile back. And it
makes me feel all warm inside.
