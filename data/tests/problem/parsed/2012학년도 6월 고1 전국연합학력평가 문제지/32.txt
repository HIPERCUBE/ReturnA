32. 다음 글의 빈칸 (A), (B)에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?
A decision­maker’s knowledge and experience are essential
and can contribute to a good decision. The decision­maker
predicts what will happen if something is done now, based
on what happened when something was done in the past.
(A) , a decision­maker uses the past to predict the
future. Being knowledgeable and experienced,
decision­makers have collected and stored much information
in their minds and thus can use this information to form a
judgment. (B) , the more knowledge and experience a
decision­maker has, the greater the chance for a good
decision.
