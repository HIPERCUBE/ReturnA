A turtle doesn’t have automatic body temperature control 
like birds and mammals. ( ① ) Its temperature changes 
according to its environment. ( ② ) When it gets too cold, 
it digs a hole deep into the mud at the bottom of a pond or 
into the dirt of the forest. ( ③ ) How can it breathe when 
it’s buried? ( ④ ) The turtle stops breathing air through its 
nose and mouth. ( ⑤ ) And when spring comes and the 
ground warms up, the turtle digs itself out and starts 
breathing normally again.
