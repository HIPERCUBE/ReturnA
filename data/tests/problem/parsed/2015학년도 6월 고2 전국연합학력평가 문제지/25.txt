American E­book Readers 
by Age Group in 2012 and 2013
The graph above shows the percentage of American people 
by age group who read at least one e­book in 2012 and 2013. 
①As a whole, the e­book reading rates in 2013 were higher 
in each age group, compared to 2012. ②The percentage gap 
between 2012 and 2013 was the smallest for the eldest group. 
③The percentage of young adults aged 18­29 who read one 
e­book or more in 2012 almost doubled in 2013. ④The e­book 
reading rates of the second youngest group increased from 
25% in 2012 to 42% in 2013. ⑤ About two out of ten 
American adults aged 50­64 read at least one e­book in 2012; 
more than half of the same age group did so in 2013.
