29. 다음 지형 그림에 대한 글의 내용 중, 밑줄 친 낱말의 쓰임이
적절하지 않은 것은?
What if you could walk across a different place in the world
every day? If you did, you might see that the land in each
place has a ① different height. Land can rise up and drop
down in many ways. A landform is a shape on the Earth’s
surface. A mountain is the ② highest landform. There aren’t
many flat places on a mountain. A landform called a plateau
can rise almost as high as a mountain. A plateau has ③ steep
sides that rise above the surrounding land. However, a
plateau has a ④ pointed top. The ⑤ lowest type of a
landform is called a plain. A plain is mostly flat or gently
rolling. It does not rise above the surrounding land.
