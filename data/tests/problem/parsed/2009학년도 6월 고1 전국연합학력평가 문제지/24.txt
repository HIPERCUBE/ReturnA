24. ‘Communifaking’ means pretending to be talking or sending
text messages on a cell phone. The most common reason is
for impression management. While waiting for friends, people
fake phone calls so that they are not seen as a lonely person.
Another reason is for . A woman told
ABCNews.com that one night at a gas station, when only one
other car was there, she pretended to talk on the phone out of
fear. “The other person at the pump was a mysterious-looking
man, I’d say, in his early 40s. So, being afraid of his talking
to me, I pretended I was talking on my phone while pumping
my gas,” she said.
