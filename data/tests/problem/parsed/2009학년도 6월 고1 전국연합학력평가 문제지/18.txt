18. 밑줄 친 this가 가리키는 것으로 가장 적절한 것은?
When you plan to make this, you have to think of several
things before you do it. First, be sure of what you want to
happen as a result of making this and prepare the right words.
Then, contact any person who will be able to give you advice
and get some to help organize your thoughts. When you make
this, remember to be calm and try not to anger the person,
because this is an expression of displeasure. It will be better if
you have sufficient evidence to support your claim.

④ a description ⑤ a presentation
