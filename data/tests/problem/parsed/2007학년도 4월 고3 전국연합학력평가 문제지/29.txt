There’s something about a spectacular landscape that makes 
my fingers itch to capture its essence on canvas. I want to create 
a landscape painting that generates the same intense emotion in 
someone who views the painting as the landscape did in me. Here 
are some tips to help you with your next landscape painting. 
You’re not obliged to include everything that you see in the 
landscape you’re painting simply because it is there in real life. 
Be selective, include the strong elements that characterize that 
particular landscape. Use the landscape as a reference, to provide 
you with the information you need to paint the elements, but 
don’t just copy it completely.
