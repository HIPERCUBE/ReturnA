36. In everyday conversation, there are different ways to say
what we want. When we are with a group of friends, we
can say to them, “Go get me that plate!” or “Shut up!”
However, when we are invited to other people’s house with
our parents, we must say, “Could you please pass me that
plate, if you don’t mind?” and “I’m sorry. I don’t mean to
interrupt, but I’m not able to hear the speaker.” We should
use different expressions in different social situations.
