34. 다음 글에서 필자의 주장으로 가장 적절한 것은?
Can you see without your eyes? There is a way of seeing
well in a different way. Close your eyes and try to feel
everything that is touching your skin. Feel the shoes on
your feet, the clothes on your body, the air on your skin.
You can see more. Go to museums. Most museums are just
for looking, but today some museums have many things to
touch. Their signs say, “Do touch!” There you can feel
everything on show. If you want to see better, reach out
and touch. Then you will really see!

③ 다른 사람을 배려해야 한다.
④ 듣는 것보다 보는 것이 낫다.
⑤ 역사에 관한 지식을 늘려야 한다.
