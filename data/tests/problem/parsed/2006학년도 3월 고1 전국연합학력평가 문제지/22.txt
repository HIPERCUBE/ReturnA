22. 다음 글에서 전체 흐름과 관계 없는 문장은?
Hollywood stars have little privacy. ① For many of them,
life is like living in a goldfish bowl．② Everyone can see
everything they do. ③ It’s not true that all stars make a lot
of money. ④They are followed by the paparazzi wherever
they go and we can read all about their private lives in
newspapers and magazines. ⑤All this means that many film
stars find it difficult to lead a normal life.
*paparazzi: 파파라치(유명 인사를 쫓아다니는 프리랜서 사진사들)
