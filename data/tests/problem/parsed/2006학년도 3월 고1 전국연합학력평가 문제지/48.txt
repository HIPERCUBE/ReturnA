Once there lived a poor family. For generations their family
job was washing clothes for others. They knew (a) a secret
for making an excellent hand cream which was very good for
chapped hands.
(A)
After the war the king made the man a noble. With
(b) the same knowledge, one man became a noble while
the family only got some money.
(B)
A man heard of this and offered a lot of money for the
secret recipe. The family discussed (c) the matter. “We
have washed clothes for others for generations,” they said,
“but we never made more than a few pennies. Today we
can sell (d) our recipe for a lot of money. Let’s sell the
idea!”
(C)
Later there broke out a war. It was winter time and the
soldiers were suffering from chapped hands. The man who
had bought (e) the idea presented it to the king of the
country. It cured the soldiers and made them strong. They
defeated the enemy completely with the help of the
wonderful cream.
*chapped: 살갗이 튼
