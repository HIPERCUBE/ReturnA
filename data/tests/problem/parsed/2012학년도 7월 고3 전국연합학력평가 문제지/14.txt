14. 대화를 듣고, 여자의 마지막 말에 대한 남자의 응답으로 가장
적절한 것을 고르시오.
Man:
① You should have made him a promise.
② He deserves it. He did the best research.
③ I don’t want to go there. It’s always over-booked.
④ I know, but the earlier you let him know, the better.
⑤ Don’t worry about it. The conference has been cancelled.
