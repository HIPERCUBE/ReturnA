30. H. Mephisto에 관한 다음 글의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?
H. Mephisto is a kind of roundworms living deep 
underground on Earth. Its name comes from Mephistopheles, 
which means “one who dislikes the light.” It was recently 
discovered even 1.3 kilometers below the ground in a South 
African gold mine. It only measures from 0.52 to 0.56 
millimeters in length, which may sound so small, but it is 
millions of times bigger than the bacteria it feeds on. It 
survives huge pressure from the ground above. In addition, it 
lives in groundwater with extremely low levels of oxygen. 
No wonder it is also called a devil’s worm.
* roundworm: 선형동물
① 빛을 싫어한다는 뜻이 이름에 담겨있다.
② 최근에 지하 1.3km 지점에서 발견되었다.
③ 자신보다 몸집이 큰 박테리아를 먹는다.
④ 고압의 환경에서도 살아남는다.
