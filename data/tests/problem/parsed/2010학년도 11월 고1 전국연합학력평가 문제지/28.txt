University has observed directly how the loss of large animals  
. When a hydroelectric 
dam flooded thousands of acres in Venezuela, Terborgh saw

support the creatures at the top of the food chain such as 
jaguars, pumas, and eagles. The disappearance of these animals 
sparked a chain of reactions. Animals, such as monkeys and
