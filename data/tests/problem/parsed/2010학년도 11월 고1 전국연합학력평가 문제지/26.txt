26.  Windows of hanok reflect the traditional view that forms 
should be created by                                   . The size and the 
placement of windows in hanok are determined by the senses, 
not by mathematical calculation. It is like building things with 
the eye, not with measuring instruments. There is no standard 
size or position for windows. They are installed where it feels 
right. This is a natural approach to creation and is based on the 
aesthetics of feelings. This characteristic of windows of hanok 
shows an example of the abstract beauty in traditional Korean 
architecture.
