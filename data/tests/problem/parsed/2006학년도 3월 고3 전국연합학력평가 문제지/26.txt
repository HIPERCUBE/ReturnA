26. In our society, the card and gift industries have come
largely to dictate when, how, and what we give for special
occasions. That’s not all bad. Many of us appreciate
remembrances for one occasion or another, such as a
birthday. But one of the peculiar effects of formalizing giving
is the sense of that has become popular.
People tend to expect certain gifts, particularly from their
partners. They may feel hurt if they don’t get them, or take
it for granted when they do. If you are the giver, it’s just
one more item on the to-do list, and in some cases, people
may even resent the necessity. [3점]
