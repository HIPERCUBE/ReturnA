Even flowers can work for us. Not only they look ① pretty
but also one kind of flowers ② is used to tell how much
smog is in the air over Tokyo. Its name is Winter Queen
Gamma 3, ③ that is a kind of begonia. ④When left out for
six days in smog, it gets white spots on its leaves. If the
smog continues for two more days, the spots turn into blisters.
Then the leaves turn brown and are filled with holes. The
flowers are working ⑤ to warn people living there of this
danger.
