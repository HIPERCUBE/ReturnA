30. 다음 글에 드러난 ‘Hector’의 심경을 가장 잘 나타낸 것은?
“You promised to buy me a suit. You said nothing about
wearing Uncle John's suit,” cried Hector. “We know, but we
can't manage it now. Since your Uncle John is the same size
as you, and he is nice enough to lend you his new suit, what's
the difference?” said his mom. Hector felt the blood rushing to
his face. His parents had been preparing his graduation party
for more than a week. ‘They should have spent the money on
my suit instead of on a dumb party,’ he thought.
