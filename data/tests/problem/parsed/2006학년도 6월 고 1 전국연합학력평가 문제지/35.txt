35. 주어진 글 다음에 이어질 글의 순서로 가장 적절한 것은?
Mayan parents wished their children to have signs of
high class.
(A) Then, the shape of the baby's head became flat and
long. They thought this long, flat head looked like
corn, which was a sign of high class.
(B) They also tied a small ball with the baby's hair so
that it fell between eyes. As the baby looked at it, he
or she later became cross-eyed. This was also a sign
of high class.
(C) They believed all people came from the corn god,
which was considered as the most important god. So
when a child was born, they put the head of the
baby between two pieces of wood for several days.
① (A) - (B) - (C) ② (A) - (C) - (B)
③ (B) - (C) - (A) ④ (C) - (A) - (B)
⑤ (C) - (B) - (A)
