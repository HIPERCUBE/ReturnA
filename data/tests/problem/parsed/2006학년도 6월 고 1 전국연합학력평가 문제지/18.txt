18. 다음 글에서 밑줄 친 It(it)이 가리키는 대상이 다른 것은?〔1점〕
We cannot see thunder, but we can hear ① it. It has the
sound of an explosion. It's like a shot from a gun. ② It has
different sounds. Sometimes ③ it sounds like clapping. Many
people are afraid of ④ it. But it cannot hurt people. Then how
does this phenomenon happen? It is the air pushed by the heat
of lightning, so it makes noises. In this way, thunder always
goes together with lightning . ⑤ It makes thunder. Light travels
faster than sound, so we see lightning before we hear it.
