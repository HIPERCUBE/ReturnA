30. 다음 도표의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?
The graph above shows changes in the numbers of  
tourists to Bali, Indonesia from four countries ― China, 
Japan, Korea, and India ― from 2002 to 2011. ① The number 
of Japanese tourists was at its peak in 2008, and decreased 
sharply from 2008 to 2011. ② On the other hand, the 
number of Chinese tourists drastically increased between 
2006 and 2011. ③ The number of the Indian tourists 
continuously increased from 2006 to 2011. ④ In 2008, there 
were less than twice as many tourists from Japan as there 
were from Korea. ⑤ From 2002 to 2008, the number of 
Korean tourists was on the rise, but the number has 
remained almost unchanged since 2008.
