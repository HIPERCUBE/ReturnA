25. 다음 글의 요지로 가장 적절한 것은?
The image of the self­sacrificing parent is often considered 
an ideal. The good father lives for his children at all costs. 
The good mother never takes a break and has no outside 
friendships or activities. Is this the ideal? In fact, the 
opposite is true. Overextended parents are often annoyed 
and depressed. Just as you can’t keep driving a car without 
ever refilling the gas, you can’t keep giving to kids without 
ever refueling emotionally. An afternoon out with friends for 
lunch can help you get refreshed. Even an hour alone in the 
tub with a good book can also make you emotionally relaxed. 
In this way, you can take care of your children with more 
energy, interest, and patience.

④ 부모는 자녀 양육을 위해 정서적 재충전이 필요하다.
