29. Logic Puzzle Museum에 관한 다음 안내문의 내용과 일치하지 
않는 것은?
Logic Puzzle Museum
The Logic Puzzle Museum is offering 
three hands­on logic puzzle sessions on 
Saturday, Dec. 8. Times are 10 am, 11 am 
and 1 pm. These sessions are for ages 
5 and above and offer more than 60 brain and logic 
puzzles to try. These are not jigsaw puzzles but 
“figure­them­out” puzzles. There are bells to ring when 
a puzzle is solved. Visitors also make a puzzle to keep. 
During the session, there is also time to see the 
exhibit of vintage puzzles. The gift shop offers various 
types of puzzles for sale. Reservations are required 
only by calling (262) 763-3946. For more information, 
go to www.logicpuzzlemuseum.org.
① 12월 8일에 3번의 퍼즐 체험 기회를 제공한다.
② 60가지가 넘는 퍼즐을 즐길 수 있다.
③ 방문객들은 직접 퍼즐을 만들 기회를 얻는다.
④ 선물 가게에서 다양한 퍼즐을 구매할 수 있다.
⑤ 홈페이지를 통해 예약이 가능하다.
