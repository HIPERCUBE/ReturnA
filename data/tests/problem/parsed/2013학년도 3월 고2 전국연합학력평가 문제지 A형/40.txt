How to Make a High ­ Bounce Ball
 What You Need:
a bottle of white glue, a glass jar, one and a half cups 
of distilled water, food coloring, a bowl, borax powder
 What to Do:
(A) While stirring, slowly pour the glue into the bowl 
containing the borax. Take the glue mixture out of 
the bowl. Push and pull on it until it’s smooth and 
dry. Roll it into a ball and make it bounce.
(B) Put 10 drops of food coloring into the jar and stir. 
Pour the remaining distilled water into the bowl. 
Add the borax powder. Stir until the powder 
dissolves.
(C) Pour the bottle of glue into the jar. Fill the 
now­empty glue bottle with 1/2 cup distilled water. 
Then pour the water from the glue bottle into the 
jar.
* distilled water: 증류수
① (A) - (C) - (B) ② (B) - (A) - (C)
③ (B) - (C) - (A) ④ (C) - (A) - (B)
⑤ (C) - (B) - (A)
