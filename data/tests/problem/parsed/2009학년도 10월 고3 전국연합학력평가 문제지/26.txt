26. A noted scientist, Ellsworth Huntington, concluded from
other men’s work and his own involving people living in
different climates, that climate and temperature have a
definite effect on our mental abilities. He found that cool
weather is much more favorable for creative thinking than
hot weather. The mental abilities of large numbers of people
tend to be lowest in the summer. Spring appears to be the
best period of the year for thinking. One reason may be that
in the spring man’s mental abilities are affected by the same
factors that bring about great changes in nature. Fall is the
next best season, then winter. As for summer, it seems to
be a good time to . [3점]
① take a vacation from thinking
② learn a foreign language
③ achieve some great results
