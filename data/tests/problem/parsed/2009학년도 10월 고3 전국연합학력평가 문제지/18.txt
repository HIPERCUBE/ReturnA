18. 밑줄 친 This[this]가 가리키는 것으로 가장 적절한 것은?
This is one of the oldest structures in architectural
history. The first kind of this was probably wood trunks
fitted together, and was used to acquire strategic positions
for survival. This was first used to overcome the difficulties
presented by the terrain, such as valleys or mountains.
People could move up as quickly as possible, which often
meant moving to a place of greater security. A modern form
of this is a mechanically moving device that carries people
between floors of a large building, enabling them to move up
or down without walking. * terrain: 지형
