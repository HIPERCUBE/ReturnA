21. 다음 글의 요지로 가장 적절한 것은?
One reason many people keep delaying things they should 
do is that they fear they will do them wrong or poorly, so 
they just don’t do them at all. For example, one of the best 
ways to write a book is to write it as quickly as possible, 
getting your thoughts onto paper without regard to style. 
Then, you can go back to revise and polish your writing. 
If I only wrote when I knew it would be perfect, I’d still 
be working on my first book! Do you have a hard time 
relaxing if your house is a mess? Do you beat yourself up 
for making mistakes? I’ve got a simple message for you 
today: It’s time to let go of your perfectionism. It becomes 
a stumbling block that keeps you stuck.
