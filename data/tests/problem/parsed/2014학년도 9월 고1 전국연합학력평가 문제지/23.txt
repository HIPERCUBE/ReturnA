DISH COMPETITION
The Art Institute of INC is hosting the “2014 Korean 
Dish Competition.” This is the third annual competition and 
we hope to build on last year’s success. The award winning 
teams will be given the opportunity to participate in the 
“2014 Korean Food Festival” on November 14th, 2014.
• When: Saturday, September 27th, 2014, 9:00 a.m.-11:00 a.m.
• Where: The Grand Hall in The Art Institute of INC
• Who: Students in Teams of 3
• Awards: Grand / Silver / Bronze prizes
• Registration: August 26th-September 9th, 2014 
e‒mail: inckdc@dmail.com
