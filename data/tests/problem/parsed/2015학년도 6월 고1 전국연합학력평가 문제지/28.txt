Eastside University Summer School
■ A seven-week program:
You’ll experience a summer of challenge, 
discovery, and growth.
■ Who should apply?
This program is open to high school students who 
will graduate in 2016.   
■ What can you expect? 
You’ll conduct research in the largest university 
library in the world. 
■ Cost:
You’ll pay from $920 to $1,140 depending on 
how many classes you register for.
