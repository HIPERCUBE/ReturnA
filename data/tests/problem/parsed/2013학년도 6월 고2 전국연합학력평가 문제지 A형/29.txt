Wire Sculpture Workshop
Have you liked playing with your hands since
childhood? Great! This is where you can develop your
skills to the fullest.
Instructor: Charles O’Neil
Dates: June 10 & 11
Fee: $90 (material fee NOT included)
Wire sculpture is an exciting, expressive art form that
can be functional or decorative.
• You will receive step­by­step instruction to
successfully create a garden goose.
• The creation is designed for indoor or garden use.
• There will be a material fee of $45 payable to the
instructor.
Please note:
This workshop requires
extensive use of hand
tools. A certain level
of manual skill is
needed.
