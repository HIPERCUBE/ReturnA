35. 다음 글에 드러난 Liz의 심경 변화로 가장 적절한 것은?
Over the sky a plump black cloud moved, erasing the 
stars. At that moment, Liz heard a stealthy step far to her 
left and immediately froze. She slowly released her knees 
and straightened her head, the better to hear. The movement 
stopped, and after a long moment began again. Liz saw a 
dark figure creep into the open and draw near to the trees. 
The figure stood so still and low to the ground that it might 
have been a stump. She called softly, “Tim!” Hearing an 
answering whistle, she went toward her son and, with tears 
of delight, hugged him tightly.
① lonely→ scared
② tense→ relieved
③ sorrowful→ ashamed
④ anticipating→ depressed
⑤ disappointed→ overjoyed
