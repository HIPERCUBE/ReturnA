25. Ironically, the Alps are becoming victims of .
In a strange twist of fate, this vast and beautiful European
mountain chain is being destroyed by the very tourists it
attracts. Every year, 20 million skiers visit the Alps. The
Alpine roads are blocked by many vacationers in traffic
jams. Off the roads, the Alpine area is threatened by the
flood of very noisy motorcycles, off­road vehicles,
snowmobiles and helicopters. Moreover, snow guns create
artificial surfaces for skiers, which breaks the weak Alpine
environment.
