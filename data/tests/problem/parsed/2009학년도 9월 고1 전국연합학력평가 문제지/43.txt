43. 다음 글의 목적으로 가장 적절한 것은?
Dinner and a movie can make you spend all your
allowance. But wise daters can still have a wonderful time
with less money. Here are some inexpensive ways to
release your inner Cupid. Prepare a meal together. It will be
more memorable for having created it as a team. Share a
hobby or other interests. Together, you can hunt for fossils
or attend a poetry reading. Romantic picnics can be set
anywhere: your backyard, a scenic park or even your living
room floor. Don’t forget the candles! Remember, money can’t
buy a good date— the best ones are those spent with
someone you love, no matter where you are.
