36. iris에 관한 다음 글의 내용과 일치하는 것은?
The iris is one of the most popular garden flowers. There
are hundreds of different kinds. These graceful flowers
bloom soon after tulips do in spring. Irises are easy to
grow. In most places, they need no protection from summer
sun or winter cold. They do not need special kinds of soil.
But the gardener who raises irises does have one job to do
about every four years. When the plants become too
crowded, they must be dug up and divided.
① 정원용 화초로 적절하지 않다.
② 꽃이 튤립보다 먼저 핀다.
③ 더위나 추위를 잘 견뎌낸다.
④ 특정한 토양에서 잘 자란다.
⑤ 4년에 한 번씩 꽃이 핀다.
