35. 다음 도표의 내용과 일치하지 않는 문장은?
Housework hours per week
This graph shows the number of hours married men and
women spend per week doing their housework. ① In
families without children, women work 30 hours per week.
② On the other hand, men do housework less than 20
hours. ③When they have children, the gap in their
housework hours becomes smaller. ④ The number of hours
women work rises to 52 per week in families with one or
two children. ⑤When there are three or more children in
the family, women work over three times longer than men
do.
