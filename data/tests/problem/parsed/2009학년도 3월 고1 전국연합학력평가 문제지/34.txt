34. 다음 글의 주장으로 가장 적절한 것은?
Good looks can be an advantage. However, you should not
complain about yourself for what you received from your
parents. Instead, you have to remember that beauty will not
last long. That’s why it is important to develop your ability
so that you will have tools for success later in life. It’s also
important to concentrate on what you can do to make the
world a better place. It is useless to worry that you are not
beautiful enough.
① 자신의 실수를 남의 탓으로 돌리지 마라.
② 불만을 말할 때는 타당한 근거를 제시하라.
③ 이웃을 위해 자신이 할 수 있는 일을 찾아라.
④ 부모에게 지나치게 의존하려는 생각을 버려라.
⑤ 외모에 대해 불평하기보다는 능력을 계발하라.
