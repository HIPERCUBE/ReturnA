18. 밑줄 친 This[this]가 가리키는 것으로 가장 적절한 것은?
This is one of the most widely grown food plants in the
world. This has many small grains, and is used as food for
farm animals as well as for humans. This is the most
important crop in the United States, which produces about
half the world’s total. In Korea, this is not a regular food
but a good snack for children. Many children hold this with
both hands while eating, which looks like playing the
harmonica.
