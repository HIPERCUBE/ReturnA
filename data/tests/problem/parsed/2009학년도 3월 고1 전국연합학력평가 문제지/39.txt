39. Francis Bacon said “Knowledge is power.” But knowledge
alone is not power. Knowledge has value only in the hands
of someone who has the ability to think well. People must
learn how to think well to achieve their dreams. David J.
Schwartz says, “Success is mainly affected by the size of
people’s thinking.” Knowledge should go hand in hand with
the ability to think well. [1점]
