29. 다음 글에 드러난 ‘I’의 심정으로 가장 적절한 것은?
The street was jammed with cheerful and happy crowds―
scenes that brought back memories of happy years that were
gone. I just couldn’t bear the thought of going home to an
empty apartment. I didn’t know what to do. I couldn’t keep
the tears back when I thought of the good times I had had
with my husband. After walking aimlessly for an hour or
so, I found myself in front of a bus terminal. I
remembered that my husband and I had often boarded an
unknown bus for adventure, so I boarded the first bus I
saw. I absent­mindedly looked out the window without
knowing where the bus was taking me.
