Production of Electronic Merchandise
of the Top Five Countries
This chart shows the changes in the total value of 
electronic merchandise produced by the top five countries 
between 2001 and 2004 in terms of US dollars. ① Korea 
showed an increase in the production of electronic 
merchandise in 2004 in comparison with that of 2001. 
② The U.S.A. was the largest producer of electronic 
merchandise both in 2001 and in 2004. ③ Notably, 
among the top five countries, China achieved the largest 
increase in amount in this period. ④ Japan also made an 
increase in electronic products, but the Japanese increase 
was smaller than that of Germany. ⑤ Each of the five 
countries produced more electronic merchandise in 2004 
than in 2001.
