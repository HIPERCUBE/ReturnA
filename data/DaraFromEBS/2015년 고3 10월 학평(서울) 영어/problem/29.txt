New Wood Student Woodworking Competition
New Wood is an industry­sponsored 
competition intended for serious woodworking 
students who are considering a career in the 
woodworking industries. There is no entry fee.
Registration Deadline: November 1, 2015
Submission Deadline: November 1, 2015
AWARD / PRIZE
Prizes are awarded in each category.
First Place - $800
Second Place - $400
Third Place – $100
⚫ Student projects may be entered in the chair, table, 
or desk categories.
⚫ Participants must be at least 16 years of age by 
October 22, 2015.
⚫ Entries are restricted to two per participant.
For more information, visit our website at 
www.nwswc.com.
① 참가비가 없다.
② 등록 마감일과 제출 마감일이 같다.
③ 부문별로 3등까지 상금을 수여한다.
④ 4개의 참가 부문이 있다.
⑤ 참가자당 작품을 2개까지 제출할 수 있다.
