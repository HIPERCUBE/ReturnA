3. 대화를 듣고, 여자의 마지막 말에 대한 남자의 응답으로 가장 
적절한 것을 고르시오.
① It’s hard to endure the loss of a cherished puppy.
② You shouldn’t have taken your eyes off the road.
③ You should break the habit of being late all the time.
④ Don’t worry. You can do it if you put your mind to it.
⑤ What a coincidence! I was about to drop by your house.
