19. 대화를 듣고, 여자의 마지막 말에 대한 남자의 응답으로 가장 
적절한 것을 고르시오. [3점]
Man:
① You have to renew your driver’s license.
② I’ll let you take the midterm a second time.
③ Then you should easily pass the test this time.
④ I don’t think memorizing is the best way to study.
⑤ No wonder you’re curious about your test results.
