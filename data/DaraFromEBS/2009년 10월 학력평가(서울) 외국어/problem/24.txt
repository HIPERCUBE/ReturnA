24. Do you know a teaching approach called “jigsaw”? The
essence of this approach requires students to work together
to master the material to be tested on an upcoming
examination. The end is accomplished by grouping students
into cooperating teams and giving each student only part of
the information necessary to pass the test. Under this
system the students must take turns teaching and helping
one another. Everyone needs each other to do well. When
tried in multiracial classrooms, this approach has generated
impressive results, compared to other classrooms using the
traditional competitive method. This method stimulated
significantly more friendship and less prejudice among racial
groups. This approach to learning has proved to be able to
.
① improve students’ memory
② relieve interracial tension
③ satisfy curiosity for nature
④ motivate interest in drawing
⑤ reduce dependence on parents
