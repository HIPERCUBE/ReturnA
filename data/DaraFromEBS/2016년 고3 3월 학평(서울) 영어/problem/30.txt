30. 다음 글에 드러난 ‘I’의 심경으로 가장 적절한 것은?
I opened the letter and started reading. I didn’t even read 
the whole thing. I read only the beginning ― where it said, 
‘We are pleased to inform you that you have received a 
scholarship that covers full tuition’ ― and I started jumping 
and running around the house. I couldn’t even believe it. I 
read the letter four more times. My dream school had 
offered me a full scholarship. That was more money than I 
could even imagine. All the stress, studying for exams, and 
staying up late to study ― it was all worth it.
