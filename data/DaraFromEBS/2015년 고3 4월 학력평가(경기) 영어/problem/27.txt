27. City Shuttle Bus에 관한 다음 안내문의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?
City Shuttle Bus 
• The free shuttle bus service operates in our historic 
port city from Central Station to Marin Education 
Center via George Street. 
• You can board the bus at any stops that display the 
green shuttle logo. 
• Each shuttle bus is accessible to people in wheelchairs.
• Buses run every 10 minutes. 
Operating Hours: 
Weekdays   7:30 a.m. - 6:30 p.m. 
Weekends   10:00 a.m. - 6:30 p.m. 
For more information, visit www.cityshuttlebus.com.
